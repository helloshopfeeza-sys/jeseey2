Recruitment Process

We ensure a transparent and smooth recruitment journey. Follow our step-by-step process to start your career abroad.

Process for Male Vacancies

1

Consultation

Initial discussion about your skills, experience, and career goals

2

Interview

Formal interview with the employer or their representative

3

Selection

If selected, you proceed to the next stage of the process

4

Sign Offer Letter

Review and sign the official job offer letter from the employer

5

Medical Checkup

Complete the required medical examination for overseas employment

6

Sign Agreement

Sign the employment agreement and contract documents

7

Receive Job Visa

Obtain your work visa for the destination country

8

SLBFE Approval

Get final clearance from Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment

FINAL
9

Departure

Begin your exciting journey to your new workplace abroad!

Our Story

Building Dreams Since Day One

Jeseey Foreign Employment Agency was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between talented individuals seeking opportunities abroad and employers looking for dedicated workers.

What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in foreign employment recruitment in Sri Lanka. Our journey has been marked by countless success stories of individuals who have transformed their lives through opportunities we helped create.

Today, we proudly serve as a licensed recruitment agency under the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment (SLBFE), maintaining the highest standards of ethical recruitment practices.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide ethical, transparent, and efficient recruitment services that connect skilled Sri Lankan workers with quality employment opportunities worldwide, while ensuring their safety, dignity, and professional growth.
