Our Story

Building Dreams Since Day One

Jeseey Foreign Employment Agency was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between talented individuals seeking opportunities abroad and employers looking for dedicated workers.

What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in foreign employment recruitment in Sri Lanka. Our journey has been marked by countless success stories of individuals who have transformed their lives through opportunities we helped create.

Today, we proudly serve as a licensed recruitment agency under the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment (SLBFE), maintaining the highest standards of ethical recruitment practices.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission
